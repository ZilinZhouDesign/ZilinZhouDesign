Zilin Zhou 周子琳

Architect X designer 建筑师 X 设计师

My mission is to craft living spaces that captivate with intrigue and poetry, resonating harmoniously with nature, animals, and the local community. Simultaneously, I am engrossed in the study of digitized design methodologies, aiming to broaden the dimensions of architectural design through interactive approaches. 我的使命是创造富有诗意的生活空间，与自然、动物和当地社区和谐共鸣。同时，我专注于数字化设计方法的研究，旨在通过交互式方法拓展建筑设计的维度。
