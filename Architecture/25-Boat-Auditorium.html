    • Boat Auditorium

    The Boat Auditorium on Shengshan Island stands gracefully on the cliff in a shape resembling a boat, continuing the cultural story of a century-old fishery.

    The rooftop boat drying theater provides a 270-degree panoramic sea view experience, visitors can enjoy the sea sunset. Indoor Cabin Auditorium has a large window to see tides, and you can feel the changes of sea water and time while enjoying music. The multifunctional hall on the ground floor stretches towards the corner of the cliff, where architecture and nature, waves and music blend together

    Sound and Nature

    Beneath the stunning scenery of Zhoushan, I envision a container nestled at the corner of cliffs surrounded by three sides of sea, harmonizing with the sky, the ocean, and nature itself.

    The design of the fishing boat is inspired by the perception of nature; land should belong to nature, so I choose to gently lift the architecture, like a boat floating on the sea, merely passing through nature.

    Cove Hall

    The ground floor's multifunctional hall resembles a hollowed-out cave, akin to the underside of a ship, with long and hefty concrete walls on both sides enclosing transparent spaces that directly connect to the natural world outside. The outdoor wooden deck platform serves as an extension of the indoor space, offering limitless potential for spatial use.

    Entrance Deck

    Ascending along the graceful steel ramp, visitors arrive at the deck entrance where a vast curved ceiling lowers the sightline. Here, through slanted openings, one can catch glimpses of the panoramic seascape, leading forward into the Cabin Auditorium.

    Cabin Auditorium

    The interior ship theatre acts as a vessel for music, absorbing the sounds of the outside world—wind, rain, and the ebb and flow of tides. Positioned in front of the stage, the windows directly face the sea. Those seated nearby can enjoy not only the music but also the changing skies, while those on the elevated tiers further back can appreciate the rhythmic movements of the waves and the shimmering reflections of light. It's an experience of watching the tides rise and fall to the serene melodies of music.

    Roof Theater

    The sky serves as the most enchanting immersive backdrop, so why not make it the theater's curtain? Here, one can indulge in a 270-degree panoramic view of the sea, basking in sunlight while witnessing the passage of time.
