    • LOOP Bamboo Pavilion

    Bamboo is a sustainable natural resource due to its fast growth rate and short harvest cycle. Storing carbon of building bamboo structures is a green way of life. Meanwhile, the texture and smell of bamboo have a unique aesthetic that allows visitors to immerse themselves.The bamboo pavilion I designed is a simple Loop art installation. It is like the bud of a life breaking out of the ground, slowly blooming itself, and finally returning to nature, forming a self-loop.

    Walking in the LOOP

    People can walk around the loop which is formed by the bending bamboos. The lifting of the two ends of the loop at different heights creates an internal corridor, like spreading wings. People can stop under the cantilevered cornice and enjoy the beauty of the bamboo forest. Different distances and perspectives will bring changing aesthetics. Beautiful arcs and pure structures can bring mental healing. The partially exposed bamboo structure in the middle allows visitors to feel the beauty of bamboo as a structure more closely. They will also have the opportunity to observe how the bamboo structure is tied.
