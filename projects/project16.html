Longquan Cable Station 龙泉揽月 - 缆车站

The Longquan Cable Car takes the shape of the madness of cursive script, and it is completed in one go on the top of the mountain. When tourists arrive at the station, they can also enjoy the whole view of Optics Valley through the platform extended from the top of the mountain. It is built on the top of the mountain and invites a bright moon, and tourists take the cable car to enter the mountain, like the realm of heaven in the poem of exile.
