Design Concept 设计理念

The design concept of this project stems from an in-depth consideration of nature and architecture. We attempt to create a space that dialogues with nature in the modern city, where architecture is not just a physical entity, but a medium connecting people with nature. 这个项目的设计理念源于对自然与建筑的深度思考。我们试图在现代都市中创造一个与自然对话的空间，建筑不仅仅是一个物理实体，更是连接人与自然的媒介。

Spatial Layout 空间布局

The spatial layout fully considers natural lighting and ventilation, creating rich spatial levels through staggered design. Each functional area is carefully planned to ensure independence while forming good spatial fluidity. 空间的布局充分考虑了自然采光和通风，通过错层设计创造出丰富的空间层次。每个功能区域都经过精心规划，既保证了独立性，又能形成良好的空间流动性。
