Ring Pavilion 九峰飞虹

In the post-pandemic era, Wuhan is undergoing a process of urban rejuvenation and rebirth, while continually balancing ecological civilization with technological advancement. Longquan Mountain and Jiufeng Mountain, as vital north-south nodes of Wuhan's central ecological corridor, constitute essential components of the city's ecology. The shaping of urban terraces on mountaintops will enable the burgeoning technological brilliance of the Optics Valley Central City to be fully appreciated.

So, what kind of future urban space should the ecologically rich Jiufeng and Longquan Mountains help Wuhan create?

后疫情时代的武汉，经历着城市的复苏与涅槃，也不断平衡生态文明与科技发展的共生关系。光谷中央生态与科技走廊的结合，必将成为影响中国城市发展策略的标杆。

龙泉山、九峰山作为武汉中央生态大走廊的重要南北节点，是城市生态的重要一环。山顶城市阳台的塑造，能够将不断崛起的光谷中心城的科技之光尽收眼底。拥有丰富生态资源的九峰、龙泉，应该为武汉营造怎样的未来城市空间呢？

Floating Spaceship 飞虹之环

As an urban terrace, the Peak Rainbow is suspended atop Jiufeng Mountain in a ring of ecological and technological essence, overlooking the Optics Valley. Inspired by the post-rainbow halo, symbolizing the rebirth of Wuhan, the design features a sleek and sturdy steel structure supporting a circular walkway, exuding a sense of space and futurism. 九峰秀列飞虹畔，俯仰云散光谷间。 作为一座城市露台，九峰飞虹悬挂在九峰山之上，环绕着生态与科技的精髓，俯瞰光谷。设计灵感来源于雨后彩虹光环，象征着武汉的重生。它采用简洁而坚固的钢结构支撑着一个环形步道，散发出空间感与未来感。

Rainbow Walkway 彩虹环道

Simultaneously, the Rainbow Loop provides hikers with forest strolls at different heights. The wooden terrace at the top of the rainbow serves as a part of the mountaintop trail, allowing hikers to freely gaze upon the future Optics Valley Center. The indoor curved viewing platform jutting out from the cliff offers excellent panoramic views and a comfortable experience. The overall lightweight construction minimizes contact with the mountain surface, aiming to protect ecological resources. 同时，彩虹环道为徒步者提供了不同高度的森林漫步体验。彩虹顶端的木质露台是山顶步道的一部分，让徒步者可以自由地眺望未来的光谷中心。从悬崖外伸的室内曲线观景平台提供了极佳的全景视野，带来舒适的体验。整体轻质结构最大程度减少了与山体表面的接触，旨在保护生态资源。

Transparent indoor Space 透明全景空间

The Feihong Grandstand, which is picked out from the cliff, provides visitors with the largest indoor viewing field, enjoying the ever-changing development of the Optics Valley, and at the same time, they can also take a break and drink tea in it. 挑出山崖的飞虹看台，为访客提供了最大的室内观景视野，欣赏日新月异的光谷发展，同时也可在其中休憩品茶。

Three Rings 三环流线

Jiufeng Rainbow is composed of three walking loops, namely the roof trail loop with the best view of Optics Valley, the outdoor trail loop walking in the annular space, and the Feihong indoor trail loop. Visitors can choose their favorite experience path according to their physical strength and needs. 九峰飞虹由三条步行环线组成，分别是可以欣赏光谷最佳视野的屋顶步道环，游走于环形空间内部的 室外步道环，以及飞虹室内步道环。访客可根据体力、需求选择自己心仪的体验路径。
