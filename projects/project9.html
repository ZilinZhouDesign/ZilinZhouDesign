Gecko Suite: Extension of the Body 蜥蜴式套装：身体的延申

2055：We are not living the normal life which our parents had and got strapped away from us. Following the global nuclear war of 2055, nature seems to be taking control and dominate over the once heavy urbanized cities.

Humans are hesitant and do not trust the ideology of a main political structure which usually forget the individual and the connections between its members. Instead, they flock in small numbers and are highly dependant on one another to survive in these new harsh conditions. Radioactivity levels are still high, especially the first ten meters above the streets, giving rise to unimaginably dangerous creatures.

This lead for one particular nomadic tribe to look upwards, not be scared of heights and thriving in previously unreachable places.The human body, weakened by previous generations of office work and sitting down, can not adapt as quick to perform according to these new demands to survive in such critical condition.

未来2055：我们生活的方式已不再是父母曾经历过的“正常生活”，这一切被从我们手中夺走。在2055年全球核战争后，大自然似乎开始掌控并主导了曾经高度城市化的地区。

人类变得犹豫不决，不再信任主流政治体系的意识形态，因为这些体系往往忽视个体及其成员之间的联系。取而代之的是，人们聚集在小群体中，彼此之间高度依赖，以便在这些严酷的环境中生存。辐射水平依然很高，尤其是距离街面十米以内的区域，孕育出了难以想象的危险生物。

因此，某个游牧部落开始仰望天空，他们不再畏惧高度，在曾经无法触及的地方生存繁衍。由于上一代人长期从事办公室工作和久坐，人体已经无法迅速适应这些新环境的生存需求，变得更加脆弱。。

Grippers 吸盘触角

Inspired by octopus tentacles, we designed tentacle units with unique contraction and expansion capabilities. These units can adjust their shape in response to environmental changes, allowing them to flexibly adhere to surfaces and provide a secure connection. Whether on smooth surfaces or complex structures, the tentacle units demonstrate high adaptability and functionality. 在章鱼触角的启发下，我们尝试设计了触角单体。这些触角单体具备独特的收缩与膨胀功能，能够根据环境的变化进行形态调整。通过这种机制，触角能够灵活地吸附在物体表面，提供稳固的连接。无论是在平滑的表面还是复杂的结构上，这种触角单体都能够自如地发挥吸附作用，展现出高度的适应性与可操作性。

Extension of the Body 身体的延申

We have equipped the body with a rigid organic skeleton, serving as armor for survival in the new era. This skeleton not only provides strong protection but also supports the growth of tentacles. The tentacles are equipped with sensory capabilities, allowing them to intelligently adhere to nearby objects and adjust according to the environment. As the body moves, the color of the tentacles changes, displaying a dynamic vitality that enhances the body's flexibility and adaptability. 我们为身体装上了坚硬的有机骨骼，作为适应新时代生存环境的盔甲。这种骨骼不仅提供强大的保护力，还承载着生长在其上的触角。触角具有智能感知功能，能够敏锐地吸附周围的物体，并根据环境变化进行调整。随着动作的变化，触角的颜色也会随之变化，展现出一种充满生命力的动感，赋予身体更加灵活与适应性的能力。

Fruit Grippers 采摘果实

This hybrid system enabled the Lagartixas to not only survive but also thrive. After a long session of fruit picking, this Lagartixa decides to take a quick moment to daydream on the day when the ground plane will be free and habitable again. All her resources are attached to her and ready to for her next destination to call home. 这个混合系统不仅让拉加提克萨（Lagartixas）得以生存，还能蓬勃发展。在一次长时间的果实采摘后，这只拉加提克萨决定短暂地放松一下，幻想着有一天地面将重新变得自由并适宜居住。她的所有资源都已经附着在她身上，准备好迎接下一个目的地，那个将成为她新家的地方。
